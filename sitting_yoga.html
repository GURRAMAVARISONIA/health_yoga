2. ardha matsyendrasana 
The word “Ardha Matsyendrasana” is derived from Sanskrit and translates as “Half Lord of the Fishes Pose”.
 The Ardha Matsyendrasana Yoga is a seated twist asana practiced to improve body posture and restore the digestive system.
